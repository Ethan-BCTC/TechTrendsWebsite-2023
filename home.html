What is Digital Identity?

Space
Digital Identity is the way a person appears to others online through various information available weather posted or Leaked

How does Digital Identity Benifit society?

The benefit of digital identity is being able to contact, communicate , meet new people, and find valuable information of others instantly.

What are some drawbacks of Digital Identity in society?

One drawback is it allows for leaks of information that could cost someone their financial or physical safety or provide the information required to digitally or physically stalk without the victim ever knowing it. For example if you sign up for Amazon Shopping and they get hacked someone out there now has your name, address, bank info, relatives, DLN, sometimes SSN, without the you knowing they have it.

What does Digital Identity Require?

This trend requires: Data Centers Any Internet Connectivity Capable Device People who feel the need to post a photo of absolutely everything, from other people to moldy food

What are some technology careers related to Digital Identity?

Database administrator Web Developer Software Engineer

What are Electric Vehicles?

Space
Electric Vehicles are vehicles that use an electric motor rather than an internal cumbustion engine like majority of vehicles. Electic vehicles rely on high amounts of electricity to be capeable to move such a high weight, to sovle this issue engineers developed a new type of battery that is made up of thousands of smaller lithium batterys. Litium batterys allow the vehicle to charge at an extreamly fast pace while retaining a large amount of that power.

How do Electric Vehicles benifit society?

The benefit of electric vehicles are their ability to lower the carbon footprint of transportation as well as lower vehicle fueling costs by half, or more.

What are some drawbacks of Electic Vehicles in society?

One drawback lithium battery production of such magnitude is very harmful to the environment and is very dangerous. Production can impact the environment in many ways such as decreasing biodiversity, increasing air polution, and decreasing freshwater supply in third world contrys. This id caused by the destruction of habitats, air pollution from the heavy machineary and crude mining methods of lithium.

What do Electic Vehicles require?

This trend requires: Head units,ECUs,Electric Drivetrains, Electric Transmission, Motors, and Lithium Batterys.

What are some careers related to Electric Vehicles?

Electrical Engineer, Lithium Miner, Iron Miner, Welder, Software Engineer, Factory/Assembly line Manager, Car Dealership Manager, Salesman, Copper Miner

What is a website of value to learn more about Elecric Vehicles?

California Air Resources Board This is an important website because it is the California government site, so it's reliable information and California was the first state to implement mandatory electric vehicles laws.

What is Renewable Energy

Space
Renewable Energy is a natural energy source that is readly replaceable without intervention of man.

How does Renewable Energy Benifit society?

Renewable energy allows us to make energy cleaner and cheaper, better for both the economy and the environment.

What are some drawbacks of Renewable Energy in society?

Although the generation of the energy is free the manufacturing and development of the harvesing methods are expensive and often out of budget for the average family.

What does Renewable Energy Require?

This trend requires: Natural Energy Havesters such as solar pannels, hydro-electic dams, wind turbines, ect.
